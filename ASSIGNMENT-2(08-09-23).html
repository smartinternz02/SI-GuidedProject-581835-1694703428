      **Data Visualization using Boxplot**
      ​
      The boxplot visualizes the distribution of 'total' car crashes.
      The boxplot reveals the presence of outliers in the data, particularly on the higher end of the car crash spectrum. Some states have significantly more car crashes than the majority.
      The median car crash count appears to be around the 20-25 range.
